I am a PhD student in Operations Research at Imperial College London (Business School), advised by Prof. Wolfram Wiesemann and Prof. Ryan Cory-Wright. My research interest lies in stochastic, robust, and distributionally robust optimization, as well as their applications in business analytics and operations management. Before joining Imperial, I obtained my Master’s degree from EPFL, Switzerland, and my Bachelor’s degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China. Besides, I spent 6 months as a research intern at Logitech Europe S.A.
